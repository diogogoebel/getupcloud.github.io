FAQ

  • For whom Getup was created?

    Getup was built for developers, startups, digital agencies and companies of all sizes looking for cloud computing services to run their sites and applications. Our platform allows you to start small and grow in the cloud as your business moves forward, without the headache of having to deal with servers and infrastructure.

  • What is the gear?

    Gear is a secure container where your application or database runs. Think on a gear as your server. Each gear has its own CPU, RAM memory, bandwidth and disc storage. Your application and database can run on a single gear (non-scalable) or in multiple gears (scalable applications).

  • How Getup scalability works?

    The auto scaling feature allows your site to spin up new gears when a traffic spike occurs, making it possible to serve virtually any amount of traffic. When the traffic spike ceases, the platform will remove automatically the additional gears. We call this horizontal scaling.

  • How much does the service cost?

    The monthly fee is based on the number of gears running and for how long each gear was running on a month. For example, if your site consumed 1 small gear for an entire month then 730h * R$ 0,122 = R$ 89,06.

  • How much does it cost to scale one application?

For the additional gears you will have 24 hours (4 gears * 6h). 730h + 24h = 754h * R$ 0,122 = R$ 91,98

  • How do I pay?

    You can pay using Paypal or credit card.

  • Do I have to pay something when informing my credit card?

  • Am I billed if my gear is on a Stop state?

    Yes, our billing system uses the time that the gears existed, not the state. If you want to stop paying, you need to remove the gears.

  • What do I need to do to cancel the service?

    Just remove your application. There is no minimum time commitment or extra charge.
